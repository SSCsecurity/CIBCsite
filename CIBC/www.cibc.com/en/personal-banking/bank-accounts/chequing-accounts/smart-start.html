Why you'll love the account that grows with you

With a CIBC Smart™ Account, enjoy all the perks of Smart Start if you’re under age 25.

Enjoy unlimited everyday banking

Get free everyday banking and enjoy unlimited debit and Interac e-Transfer® transactions1,2 in Canada and no monthly fee until age 25.3

Do it all on your phone

Tap your phone to pay with Interac debit, Apple Pay® or Google Pay™ and access your account with our award-winning Mobile Banking® App.

Get student deals and discounts

Students 14 and up save up to 30% on over 450 top brands when shopping online or in-store with a free SPC+ membership.‡

Get set for school and beyond

Track your spending to maximize your savings

With CIBC Insights, we help you see where your money is going and how to save more every month.

Send money to friends and get paid faster

Split the bill with friends or send money across the country with free unlimited Interac e-Transfer transactions.2

Trade your way to the future you want

Enjoy free online North American stock and ETF trading for young adults 18 to 24 with CIBC Investor’s Edge Opens in a new window..8 There’s no minimum balance and you’ll pay no annual fees.8

Save up to 10 cents per litre on gas4

Hit the road for less and save on gas at participating locations4 when you link and use your CIBC debit or credit card with Journie Rewards.

Will you be 25 or older when you graduate? Continue to pay no monthly fee for as long as you study full time with CIBC Smart™ for Students.5

CIBC Smart Start at a glance

Key features


Special offer 

Earn up to $150† when you open your first chequing account with Smart Start. Learn how to earn $150 with CIBC Smart Start..

  • No monthly fee until you turn 25 on a CIBC Smart Account3
  • Free unlimited Interac e-Transfer transactions2
  • Free unlimited debit and Visa* Debit purchases, withdrawals and bill payments
  • One free non-CIBC ATM withdrawal in Canada per month6
Fees and details

How do I qualify?

CIBC Smart Start:

  • You must be a Canadian resident under age 25
  • You’ll need one piece of government-issued photo ID

Teens (13 to 15)

  • Open an account at any branch with or without parental signing authority.

Teens (16 to the age of majority7)

  • Open an account online or at any branch, with or without parental signing authority.

Young adults (age of majority7 to 24)

  • Apply online or meet with us at any branch.
