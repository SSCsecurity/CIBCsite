Simplify your banking

Pre-authorized payments

Pre-authorized payments are automatically debited from your chequing account monthly, semi-monthly, biweekly or weekly. They’re easy to set up and you'll never miss a bill payment again.

AutoSave

Set up automatic transfers in less than 1 minute between your CIBC chequing and savings accounts using CIBC Online or Mobile Banking® and keep your savings goal on track.

Direct deposit

Direct deposit is a quick and easy way to get your payroll or other deposits straight into your chequing account.
